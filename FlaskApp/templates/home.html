Note that while this classifier could likely be improved, the performance of any classifier on this data is inherently limited, because the class/document relationship is much weaker here than it would be in other document classification scenarios. There is nothing keeping someone from talking about watercolors in a thread in r/guns, for example.

Interestingly, while stop-word removal significantly improves accuracy, an attempt at integrating the Porter de-stemming algorithm had a (small) negative effect.
